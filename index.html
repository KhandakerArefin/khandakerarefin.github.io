About Me

I am currently an engineer at Ulkasemi Pvt. Ltd. with experience designing standard cells, memory layouts and hierarchical blocks across multiple GlobalFoundries technologies. My research interests lie at the intersection of artificial intelligence and hardware: building edge accelerators for neural networks, exploring novel floating‑point arithmetic and creating low‑power VLSI circuits. I aspire to join a PhD program to deepen my understanding of AI hardware and high‑performance computing.

Research Interests

My recent projects focus on AI hardware accelerators, developing application‑specific integrated circuits (ASICs) for microcontrollers, designing efficient floating‑point units for neural networks, and investigating low‑power VLSI techniques for future computing systems. I am also passionate about high performance computing, machine learning and embedded systems.
